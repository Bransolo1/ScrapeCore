# Task Prompt 10: Investor and Enterprise Buyer Narrative

**Role:** All specialist roles
**Input:** Full project knowledge

---

Produce an investor and enterprise buyer narrative for the product.

It must explain:
1. Why behavioural science plus AI is different from generic LLM summarisation
2. What the product does in plain English
3. Why buyers should trust it
4. Why the workflow fits regulated environments
5. The likely commercial wedge
6. Why this can become a platform rather than a one off tool

Write it in a way that can be reused for deck copy, website copy, and enterprise sales messaging.
