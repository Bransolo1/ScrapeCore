# Evaluation Rubric

Use this every time Claude produces a major deliverable.

Score each section from 1 to 5. If any category scores below 4, revise before using the output downstream.

---

## 1. Strategic Specificity
Does the output go beyond generic SaaS language and show concrete understanding of the market, user, and workflow?

**Score: /5**

---

## 2. Behavioural Science Depth
Does it use behavioural science meaningfully rather than cosmetically?

**Score: /5**

---

## 3. Evidence Hygiene
Does it clearly separate observed fact, inference, and speculation?

**Score: /5**

---

## 4. Product Realism
Could the proposed workflow and feature set actually be built and used?

**Score: /5**

---

## 5. Enterprise Readiness
Does it address security, governance, privacy, explainability, and procurement realities?

**Score: /5**

---

## 6. UX Clarity
Does it describe a user experience people could understand and trust?

**Score: /5**

---

## 7. Technical Plausibility
Does the architecture make sense without relying on magic AI assumptions?

**Score: /5**

---

## 8. Differentiation
Does it explain why this beats a generic LLM workflow or existing competitor?

**Score: /5**

---

## 9. Actionability
Could a founder, PM, designer, or engineer use this output immediately?

**Score: /5**

---

## 10. Red Team Resilience
Would the output survive a serious internal critique?

**Score: /5**
